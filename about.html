Bonovira was founded in Budapest to help organisations create healthier, more sustainable workplaces with data-driven hydration experiences.

Our story

Born in Budapest, built for workplaces across Europe.

2019 — Concept & research

Bonovira’s founders partnered with local universities to study hydration behaviour inside hybrid offices and shared workspaces.

2021 — Platform launch

The first connected dispensers shipped with telemetry, analytics, and the inaugural wellbeing challenges for early customers.

2023 — Regional expansion

Managed service teams opened in Prague and Vienna, supporting enterprise deployments across Central Europe.

Today — Scaling impact

Bonovira partners with facilities and ESG leaders to deliver measurable sustainability and wellbeing results at scale.
