Built in Budapest for workplaces everywhere

We founded Bonovira to close the gap between employee wellbeing ambitions and the operational reality of running multi-site workplaces. Our team blends product design, data science, and hospitality expertise to create hydration experiences that stand out.

Your dedicated support network

Instead of spotlighting a few executives, we invest in multidisciplinary teams that guide every stage of your hydration programme.

Implementation strategists

Workplace consultants coordinate surveys, rollout plans, and stakeholder onboarding to ensure the first day with Bonovira feels effortless.

Data & insights specialists

Analysts translate usage signals into actionable recommendations and ESG evidence, tailoring dashboards to your reporting cadence.

Field service partners

Our regional technicians and hospitality partners deliver proactive maintenance, flavour programme updates, and training for on-site ambassadors.
