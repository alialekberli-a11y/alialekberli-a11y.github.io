Hydration infrastructure designed for modern workplaces

Bonovira brings together smart dispensers, rich analytics, and managed services to create measurable wellbeing and sustainability outcomes.

Connected dispensers

Elegant hardware that delights employees while streaming telemetry for precise maintenance, stocking, and sustainability tracking.

Modular configuration

Countertop, freestanding, and integrated models with still, sparkling, and vitamin-rich options to match each workspace.

Telemetry built in

Flow sensors, filter health, and CO₂ canisters are monitored remotely so you know what needs attention before anyone notices.

Accessibility forward

Illuminated touch targets, bottle clearance alerts, and hydration prompts support inclusive use across your campus.

Analytics & insights

One dashboard for facilities, HR, and sustainability teams to understand how hydration is powering wellbeing and ESG commitments.

Real-time monitoring

Heatmaps identify the busiest locations, while alerts flag unusual patterns so teams can adapt faster.

Goal tracking & reporting

Track refill streaks, plastic saved, and CO₂ avoided with exports aligned to CSRD and GRI frameworks.

Data integrations

Send hydration milestones to Slack, Microsoft Teams, Workday, and ServiceNow to embed insights where work happens.

Employee engagement programs

Keep hydration top of mind with experiences that motivate teams and celebrate consistent healthy habits.

Onboarding journeys

Automations welcome new joiners, introduce refill stations, and set first-week streak goals linked to wellness benefits.

Team challenges

Friendly competitions with leaderboards and sustainable rewards encourage cross-team collaboration and movement.

Personalised nudges

Time-of-day reminders adapt to schedules and hydration goals, supporting hybrid employees wherever they work.
